# TechCorp Company Policies

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility
- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
- Refunds are available for annual subscriptions only (monthly subscriptions can be cancelled but not refunded)
- Partial refunds may be issued for unused portions of annual plans

### Process
1. Submit a refund request through your account settings or contact support
2. Our team will review your request within 3-5 business days
3. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days

### Exceptions
- Refunds are not available for:
  - Custom enterprise implementations
  - Third-party add-ons purchased through our marketplace
  - Accounts terminated due to terms of service violations

## Privacy Policy Summary

### Data Collection
We collect the following types of data:
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage data (features used, login times, device information)
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

### Data Usage
Your data is used to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Send important updates about your account
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

### Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- Service providers who help us operate our platform
- Legal authorities when required by law
- Business partners with your explicit consent

## Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted files: Permanently removed after 30 days in trash
- Account deletion: All data removed within 90 days of request
- Backup retention: 180 days for disaster recovery purposes
